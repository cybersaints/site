About Us

As CyberSaints we strive for more than just academic excellence, we strive to set an example for other student at our campus and around our community. We are a student-lead organization focused on improving our knowledge about a multitude of topics, which are listed below:

  • Cyber Security
  • Database Design
  • Network Design
  • Business Analytics
  • Mobile App Development
  • PC Trouble Shooting
  • System Analysis and Design
  • Microsoft Office
  • Application Development

    • We attempt to apply our knowledge at USITCC Regional and National competitions. In these competitions we compete in the subjects listed above. At these competitions we also network with companies to try to get internship and job opportunities.

    Aside from learning and competing we also strive to help and give back to our community. At Our Lady of the Lake we collaborate with our Student Government Association to help them out with any community events they have, and on our part, we help run CyberPatriot competitions for High School students in San Antonio.
